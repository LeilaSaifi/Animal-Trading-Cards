Liger

A liger is the result of breeding a male lion to a tigress.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo tigris
  • Average Length: 9.8-11.8 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 13-18 years
  • Habitat: parks, zoos, or animal sanctuaries
Roughly only 100 Ligers exist and they only exist in captivity, like parks, zoos, or animal sanctuaries because, in the wild, these species do not share the same habitat. Lions and tigers do not really have an opportunity to mate outside of captivity.
